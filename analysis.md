## 📊 Analysis Summary

This project focuses on identifying patterns and key factors contributing to hospital readmissions within 30 days.

### 🔍 Key Observations
- Elderly patients show higher readmission rates  
- Patients with chronic conditions are at greater risk  
- Prior admissions strongly correlate with readmission likelihood  
- Shorter hospital stays may indicate premature discharge  

### 📈 Insights
The analysis suggests that patient history and chronic disease management play a critical role in reducing readmissions.

### 🏥 Conclusion
Healthcare organizations can use these insights to improve discharge planning, patient monitoring, and overall care quality.
